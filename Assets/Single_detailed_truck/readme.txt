This package contains a single detailed truck with trailer.
All wheels and the trailer-doors are animatable.
Just drag the prefabs into your scene and arrange them as you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that contains all the prefabs.

3 Different Texture-Sets for the truck and trailer color are included.

Textures-sizes between 1024x1024 to 2048x2048.
Truck and trailer (each part) uses about 30.000 polygons.

The original FBX-file is also included in the package.
